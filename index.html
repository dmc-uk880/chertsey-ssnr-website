About The Club

The Chertsey SS&NR Club is a private members social club, honouring the heritage of the Soldiers, Sailors, & National Reserve. We provide a welcoming environment for all our members.

Our facilities are perfect for socialising and private functions, featuring a well-stocked bar and a traditional atmosphere. We are easily accessible with a private car park.
